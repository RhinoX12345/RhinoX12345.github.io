“This is it, Madeline. Just breathe. You can do this.”
Madeline is the main protagonist of Celeste, a brave and introspective young woman who decides to climb Celeste Mountain as a personal challenge. Though she struggles with anxiety, self-doubt, and feelings of inadequacy, her journey is one of self-discovery and emotional growth. Madeline is determined, compassionate, and deeply human, making her story both relatable and inspiring.
